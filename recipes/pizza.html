Pizza

Struggling to find something to eat? You can't go wrong with a cheese pizza.

This dish is loved by many and for good reason. It's easy to make with simple ingredients that you probably have in your kitchen. This recipe isfamily friendly so your kids or spouse can partake in it.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Red sauce
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Roll out your freshly made, or store bought dough. Once rolled out fold a small piece of dough all around the edge to form a crust
  3. Pour 4 tablespoons of the red sauce you have chosen into the center of the rolled out dough and spread evenly.
  4. If you need to finely chop your garlic then now is the time. If you already have minced garlic then even spread a light coat on top of the pizza.
  5. Add the desired amount of cheese on top of everything
  6. Use a light coat of olive oil around the crust you made earlier.
  7. Place pizza directly on the middle rack and bake for 12-15 minutes
  8. Enjoy
